How to Use

  1. Choose your preferred unit system using the toggle switch (Metric or Imperial)
  2. Measure the horizontal distance from the tree:
    • Stand on level ground
    • Measure from your position to the center of the tree trunk
    • Use a measuring tape or laser distance meter for accuracy
  3. Choose your measurement method:
    • Angle method (recommended):
      • Use a clinometer or angle-measuring app
      • Hold the device at eye level
      • Aim at the top of the tree
      • Record the angle in degrees
    • Line-of-sight method:
      • Use a laser distance meter
      • Measure the direct distance to the tree top
      • Ensure steady aim for accuracy
  4. Enter your measurements and click Calculate

Tips for Accurate Measurements

  • Choose a clear line of sight to the tree top
  • Stand at least as far from the tree as it is tall
  • Take multiple measurements and average them
  • Avoid measuring in strong winds
  • Account for sloped ground when measuring base distance
